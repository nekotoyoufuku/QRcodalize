Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how our app ("the App") collects, uses, and shares user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that any personal information collected is handled in a secure and responsible manner.

1. Information We Collect

The App provides QR code creation and saving functionality but does not collect any personal information from users. Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (such as name, email address, or phone number)
  • Location data
  • Other personal data stored on the device

2. How We Use Information

The App is designed to allow users to generate and save QR codes. The information input by users (e.g., URLs or Wi-Fi credentials) is used solely for the purpose of generating QR codes. This data is never transmitted outside of the app or stored on any external servers.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share any personal information with third parties. All data generated or saved by the user remains on their device and is not uploaded to any external systems.

4. Data Storage

Any QR codes created or saved using the App are stored locally on the user's device. The App does not store data on cloud servers or external databases. Users can manage and delete their data at any time directly from their device.

5. Security

The App is designed so that no user data is transmitted externally, minimizing the risk of data breaches. All data remains under the user's control and is stored only on their device.
